Theory Research

\(O(1)\) Prophet Inequality for Subadditive Combinatorial Auctions

Advised by: Matthew Weinberg

  • - Proved results regarding previous SOTA techniques in showing impossibility of \(o(\log \log m)\) and \(o(\log m)\) guarantees.
  • - Determined relationship between fractionally subadditive valuation functions and \((\alpha, \beta)\) balanced valuations.
  • - Proved that the constant factor guarantee of bundled prices is inextensible to the Bayesian setting.

Prophet Inequalities for Subadditive Combinatorial Auctions

Advised by: Matthew Weinberg and Huacheng Yu

  • - Surveyed constructive posted price mechanisms achieving state of the art \(O(\log \log m)\) and \(O(\log m)\) bounds.
  • - Presented new work on the existence of a constant factor prophet inequality.

Machine Learning Research

A Simple Framework for Intrinsic Reward-Shaping for RL using LLM Feedback

Advised by: Sanjeev Arora

  • - Developed an LLM-based framework for generating and refining intrinsic reward functions in RL agents, with various RL applications, including non-distributed training environments.
  • - Introduced three innovative methods for incorporating reward-shaping feedback in RL algorithms, including tabular Q-learning, deep Q-learning, and PPO.
  • - Conducted comprehensive evaluations on gym-retro environments and Pokemon Showdown, demonstrating the superiority of our LLM-informed approach over traditional deep RL and heuristic methods.

Robustification of Natural Language Proof Generation with Verifier Guided Search

Advised by: Danqi Chen

  • - Finetuned T5-Small and used BFloat16 in training the prover and implemented diverse beam search for decoding.
  • - Provided alternative pseudo negative sampling techniques and performed ablations studies in training the verifier.

LeCaR Caching with Multi Armed Bandits

  • - Developed a variant of LeCaR caching algorithm with reinforcement learning, MAB, and multiplicative weights technique.
